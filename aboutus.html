who we are?


We are a Dublin-based family business passionate about horticulture and gardening. Since 2010, we have operated and thrived out of our Mount Merrion base, offering a wide variety of servies, for both commercial and residential clients, such as interior and exterior landscaping, designing green spaces and parks, garden maintenance and much more. We believe you should love your garden, and enjoy looking out with pride upon it. Gardening offers a vast array of holistic benefits, promotes positive mental and physical health and is good for the soul. This is where we come in. Our mission is to keep your garden looking fantastic year-round. With such a wealth of experience behind our team, we made the decision to expand our enterprise and diversify into a niche and innovative gardening solution, plant supports!
